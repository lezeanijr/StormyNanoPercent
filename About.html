The Anatomy of Boxing Gloves

Let’s break down the essential components of boxing gloves:

  • Padding

    The core purpose of boxing gloves is to protect your hands and wrists. The thick padding absorbs impact, reducing the risk of fractures and sprains during training and fights.

  • Closure System

    Echelon gloves feature secure hook-and-loop closures or lace-up designs. The closure ensures a snug fit and prevents slippage during intense workouts.

  • Thumb Attachment

    The thumb is anchored to prevent accidental bending or hyperextension. This design minimizes the risk of thumb injuries.

  • Ventilation

    Proper airflow keeps your hands cool and dry. Our gloves incorporate breathable mesh panels to enhance comfort.

  • Weight

    Boxing gloves come in various weights, typically ranging from 8 oz to 16 oz. Heavier gloves offer more protection, while lighter ones allow for faster movement.

    • Purpose-Specific Styles

  • Training Gloves

  • Sparring Gloves

    Designed specifically for controlled sparring sessions, these gloves have extra padding to safeguard both you and your partner.

  • Competition Gloves
